Sydney Trains Data Visualisation System

×
Previous Next
Features:
  • Major trends of the whole train's network can be identified in the Current Network Performance
  • The scheduled and real-time performance is compared in the Time-Distance Diagram
  • The patterns of delay for different times in each station are displayed in the Arrival Variance Analysis
  • Specific queries like "Most Frequently Late Trips" and "Number of Trains that Skipped Stops" can be searched due to the interpretation of historical trains data
  • The entire real-time train's network can be seen in the Live Map due to the integration of Leaflet.js while bottleneck can be observed instantly when happened
  • Datasets can be downloaded as CSV format
  • Utilizing HTML, CSS, jQuery, Node.js, MySQL, Chart.js, Leaflet.js etc.
